why voicely

Everyone else sends your voice to the cloud.
We never let it leave your Mac.

Wispr Flow takes screenshots of your screen and ships them to its servers. Voicely runs the speech model on your own machine. It works on a plane, in a tunnel, and when their servers are down.

private by design

Your audio is processed on your device and then it is gone.

No screenshots, no telemetry, no account required to dictate. With AI cleanup on, only the text (never the audio) is sent to your own AI key, with zero-retention routing. Turn cleanup off and nothing leaves the machine at all.

EN ⇄ עב Real bilingual dictation

Speak Hebrew, English lands at your cursor. Speak English, get Hebrew. No other system-wide dictation app does it. Built for Israel and everyone who lives between two languages.

native Not Electron

A real Mac app: a few megabytes of RAM, a global hotkey, types straight into any window. It will not freeze your editor or sit there eating 800MB.

how it works

Hold a key. Speak. It lands where your cursor is.

Tap to toggle or hold to push-to-talk, in any app. Pick a mode: clean it up, polish it, turn it into a prompt, or translate it.
